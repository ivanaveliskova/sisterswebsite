Since their arrival in the United States in 1906 at St. Lucy’s Parish, Newark, NJ,
the Sisters of St. John the Baptist, whether in inner-city neighborhoods or
 suburban parishes, have served God’s people through:

              Education: 
                  (Elementary, high school, college)

              Nursing Home  /  Care for the Elderly

 

    
     Missionary Service

     Parish Ministry

     Counseling, Social Work
